Zenith Leisure Holidays

Established in 1997, Zenith Leisure Holidays Ltd. stands as a premier travel company in India, offering a comprehensive range of travel services at competitive prices. With over two decades of industry expertise, we've earned a reputation for reliability and excellence, becoming the preferred choice for travelers nationwide.

Our ethos revolves around the belief that The Journey Is More Important Than The Destination. At Zenith, our priority is to ensure a comfortable and enjoyable journey, coupled with memorable experiences for all our clients. From the moment our clients start their journey until its conclusion, we are committed to providing top-notch service and support. Our offerings cater to individuals, groups, students, honeymooners, corporate executives, and seniors alike.

MICE

Originally focused on the MICE segment, Zenith has since diversified into Events, Leisure Travel, Foreign Exchange, Inbound Travel, and Rewards & Recognition programs. Guided by industry experts in hospitality, hotels, and travel, we've maintained our position as one of India's leading travel companies.

Our success is indebted to the satisfaction of countless guests nationwide. As we move forward, Zenith remains dedicated to upholding the same high standards and nurturing long-term relationships with our valued customers for generations to come.

About Zenith Holidays

  • The Journey Is More Important Than The Destination
    Founded in 1997 as a MICE (Meetings, Incentives, Conferences, Exhibitions) travel company, now diversified into Leisure Travel, Creative Marketing, Events, Inbound Travel, E-Business, Forex, and DMC Business.
  • Vision and Mission of Zenith Holidays
    To be a market leader with revolutionary technology, delivering value and delightful travel experiences globally with a committed, empowered team.
  • Why Choose Zenith Holidays?
    Offering a wide range of holiday packages—custom or fixed—it meets the travel needs of every Indian traveler segment.
  • Offers at Zenith Holidays
    Hassle-free packages with hotels, transfers, sightseeing, meals, and tailored experiences. Destinations include Andaman, Kerala, North East, and more.
  • Book Amazing Packages
    Use filters on our website or get expert support to easily book customized or pre-designed packages online or offline.
  • Travel Insurance
    For your peace of mind, Zenith Holidays offers travel insurance packages, ensuring compliance with mandatory requirements for entry into many countries. Discover the joy of seamless travel with Zenith Holidays. Plan your ideal holiday effortlessly and start unforgettable journeys with us.
